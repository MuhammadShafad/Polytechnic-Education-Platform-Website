Download Syllabus
Students need to earn a certain number of activity points through extracurricular and co-curricular activities to be eligible for their diploma.

Common Categories for Activity Points

1. Technical Activities

● Participation in Hackathons, Coding Contests, and Project Competitions

● Attending Workshops, Seminars, and Industrial Visits

● Internships and Industrial Training

● Paper Presentations and Publications

2. Sports and Cultural Activities

● Participation in College/Inter-College Sports Competitions

● Cultural Events (Music, Dance, Drama, Art, etc.)

● NCC/NSS Activities

3. Social & Community Services

● Blood Donation Camps

● Environment Awareness Programs

● Volunteering in NGOs and Social Services

4. Entrepreneurship & Leadership Activities

● Organizing Technical and Non-Technical Events

● Student Club Activities

● Participation in Startup Events and Entrepreneurship Programs

5. Online Certifications and Courses

● Completion of Online Courses (Coursera, Udemy, NPTEL, etc.)

● Earning Certifications from Recognized Platforms

6. Other Activities

● Publishing Blogs or Technical Articles

● Working on Open Source Projects

● Contributing to College Magazines or Newsletters
